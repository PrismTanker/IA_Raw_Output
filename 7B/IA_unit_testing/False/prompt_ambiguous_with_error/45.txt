def triangle_area(side_1, side_2):
    """
    Calculate the area of a triangle based on the lengths of its two sides.
    :param side_1: The length of one side of the triangle.
    :param side_2: The length of the other side of the triangle.
    :return: The area of the triangle.
    """
    # Calculate the length of the third side
    side_3 = (side_1**2 + side_2**2)**0.5

    # Calculate the area of the triangle using Heron's formula
    area = (side_1 * side_2 * side_3) / 2

    return area
```
